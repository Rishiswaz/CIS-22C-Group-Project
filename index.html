    About

    The program is a fantasy sports draft predictor, based on player stats and the value of each position the program will suggest someone for you to pick up.

    What can I do

    You can sort through your team, search a list of top fantasy players, and step up your draft. It doesn't pick for you, if just suggests so if you don't like the pick, just say you don't want that player and it will spit out another.
